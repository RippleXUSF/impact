Financial Inclusion

The Problem

Sustainability & Carbon Markets

Bitcoin uses as much energy as some countries. Ripple took a fundamentally different approach: the XRP Ledger uses a consensus protocol instead of mining, making it 99.99% more energy efficient than proof-of-work blockchains.

Ripple has been carbon neutral since 2020 — the first major blockchain to achieve this. They then committed $100 million to modernize global carbon markets using blockchain, tokenizing 182,000 metric tons of carbon credits to make sustainability measurable and tradeable.
