Real-World Impact
01
Banking · Europe & Asia
Santander & SBI Holdings
Santander's One Pay FX sends money from the UK to Spain in seconds. SBI Holdings uses XRP across Asia. Traditional remittances cost 5–10% in fees — Ripple brings that under 1%, saving families hundreds of dollars a year.
02
Humanitarian Aid · Jordan & Bangladesh
UN World Food Programme
Over 1 million refugees access food using iris scans tracked on blockchain — no bank account needed. $555M distributed, 25M transactions processed, $3.5M saved in fees. Tamper-proof records that corrupt officials can't touch.
03
Disaster Response · Nepal
Rahat — UNICEF Partnership
When floods hit Nepal in 2024, the Rahat platform delivered cash to 774 people with disabilities in under 6 hours. Traditional aid takes days. Over 80,000 lives impacted to date.
04
Property Rights · Kenya
Blockchain Land Registry
In Kenya, land records are paper-based and easily corrupted. Atlas Kenya and Kenya Land LayBy put titles on blockchain — ownership can't be altered without your cryptographic signature, giving marginalized communities legal protection and access to credit.
05
Supply Chain · Bangladesh
UNICEF Vaccination Tracking
Every dose, every child — recorded immutably. Blockchain prevents double-counting, ensures supply chain integrity, and helps officials identify exactly where coverage gaps exist. No more guessing. No more fraud.
